About

Hi! My name is Abhinav Jha and I am a mathematician working currently in University of Stuttgart. Broadly, I work in Numerical Analysis and Scientific Computing. During my PhD, my area of expertise was Stabilization techniques for Convection-Dominated problems. I was also a member of the Berlin Mathematical School (BMS). During my PostDoc, I switched my area and started working in computational chemistry. Here my area of work is Domain decomposition method for Poisson-Boltzmann equations. If you want to have a brief overview of my work, here is a presentation for the AFC schemes, for the ddLPB method, and for the ddPB method.

    Numerical Analysis

  • Numerical Analysis, Richard L. Burden, J. Douglas Faires: Covers all the sections of introductory Numerical Analysis

  • A Posteriori Error Estimation in Finite Element Analysis, J. Tinsley Oden and Mark Ainsworth: Covers important topics in Adaptive FEM
